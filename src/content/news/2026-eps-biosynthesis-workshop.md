Spent an afternoon at the EPS workshop on biosynthesis of natural products, organised by [Esteban Charria Girón](https://www.linkedin.com/in/echarria/), [Barbara Terlouw](https://www.linkedin.com/in/barbara-terlouw-7893534b/), [Joelle Mergola-Greef](https://www.linkedin.com/in/joellemg/) and [David Meijer](https://www.linkedin.com/in/davidmeijerbif/).

![The workshop crowd](/assets/news/eps-biosynthesis-workshop/group.jpeg)

We worked through different chemistries — Type I PKS, Type I NRPS, RiPPs and terpenes. Hands-on PKS/NRPS session with [Barbara](https://www.linkedin.com/in/barbara-terlouw-7893534b/) was the highlight for me!

The other highlight was [Esteban](https://www.linkedin.com/in/echarria/)'s [SpecReBoot](https://github.com/ECharria/SpecReBoot) ([preprint](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.64898/2026.02.03.703446v2)): bootstraps meet molecular networking. A quite elegant, phylogeny-inspired solution to the problem of reliable connections between nodes ;)
